Security, Privacy, and Safe Chat Practices

Security is paramount when participating in **live video chat with strangers**. We want you to feel completely secure on QuikChat.

Our Technology and Safety Measures

Your conversations on QuikChat are protected by cutting-edge technology. **WebRTC connections are Peer-to-Peer (P2P)**, meaning your video and audio data travels directly between your device and your partner's device. Our server only handles the initial "signaling" (the handshake) to set up the connection. This P2P architecture ensures your media stream is not stored or routed through our main servers, offering a high degree of privacy and protection against eavesdropping.

Instant Reporting and Moderation

We encourage a respectful and safe environment. If you encounter any user engaging in inappropriate behavior (such as abuse, nudity, or spam), our **Instant Report Button** allows you to flag the user immediately. Once reported, our system is notified, and the connection is terminated, allowing you to quickly move to the next conversation by clicking 'Next'. We strictly enforce our Terms of Service to maintain a clean platform for all users seeking genuine **random chat** experiences.
